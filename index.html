Our Story

In the heart of a bustling city, a group of friends, united by their shared concern for the environment, found themselves grappling with a unique challenge - how to raise awareness about the silent threat of ocean acidification. Determined to make a difference, they decided to channel their passion into action.

Late one night, during an animated brainstorming session, someone proposed the name "pHighters." The wordplay on "pH," which represents the acidity level, and the phonetic similarity to "fighters" struck a chord. It perfectly encapsulated our mission to combat ocean acidification. The name "pHighters" became more than just a clever play on words; it embodied the spirit of the team's fight against an invisible enemy threatening the oceans.

As we delved into our work, the name became a rallying cry for the group, resonating with the broader community. "pHighters" became synonymous with a collective effort to raise awareness, innovate solutions, and fight for the health of the oceans - a name that not only reflected our mission but also inspired others to join the cause.

Our Product

1st step: Collection of acidified ocean water in a segregated chamber.

2nd step: Collected ocean water will be passed through a column of alkaline solution like sodium hydroxide to regulate the pH level.

3rd step: Filtering off the insoluble residual salts through membrane filters.

4th step: Treated ocean water will be drained back carefully to the oceans.

5th step: The residual salts like sodium carbonate further treated and segregated to be used as raw material for various industries.
